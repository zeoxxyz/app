About us

Tools built for serious Luau devs

ZEOX started as an internal tool for a small group of Roblox scripters who were tired of watching cheap obfuscators get reversed in seconds. Today it's a full VM-based protection engine trusted by hundreds of developers.

Our approach

Strong. Transparent. Fast.

Protection shouldn't be a black box, and it shouldn't slow you down.

01

Register-based VM

ZEOX rewrites your script into bytecode that runs on a virtual machine bundled with it. The original source never ships — reversers see VM state, not logic.

02

Zero trust

Every string, table and control-flow branch is transformed. Even if attackers dump memory mid-run, they only see opaque instructions.

03

Developer-first UX

Clean dashboard, real usage stats, keyboard shortcuts and instant feedback. Protection shouldn't get in the way of shipping.

04

Fair pricing

Free daily quota for everyone, honest Pro tiers for power users. No hidden limits, no surprise charges.
